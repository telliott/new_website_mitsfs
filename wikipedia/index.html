Flood Facts
  • The flood was caused by a pipe burst in Stratton Center on February 4, 2023.
  • The shelf collection was largely unharmed as the flood did not rise higher than the bottom of the shelves.
  • The flood caused many donations that had not been processed or were in Niagracomm to have to be thrown out as they were on the floor and water seeped into the boxes.
  • Books and magazines were returned from storage in March 2024 and new shelving was put into place to receive them at that time.
  • The collection came back on 37 pallets that had to be cataloged, processed and reshelved in a new layout.
  • The library officially reopened from the aquapocalypse on April 4, 2025.
  • Hours are still reduced due to a limited number of keyholders. Students wanting to become keyholders are welcome!
